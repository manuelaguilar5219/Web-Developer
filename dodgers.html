Baseball

Favorite Team

My favorite baseball team is the Los Angeles Dodgers. I grew up going to games at Dodger Stadium, I always enjoy going to games with my family. Basically my whole family from my dad's side are Dodger fans.

My Grandpa, dad, uncle, and brothers are all Dodger fans so, it is cool being able to talk to them about something that we are all very passionate about.

Favorite Players

My favorite players right now on the Dodgers are Shohei Ohtani and Mookie Betts. There are a lot of great players on the Dodgers but these two are the best players on the team.

Some other notable players that I like are Freddie Freeman, Will Smith, Tyler Glasnow and Clayton Kershaw who is one of the best pitchers of all time.

My favorite player growing up was probably Adrián González. We traded for him in 2012 and he played with the dodgers until 2017.

Stadium

The Dodgers have played at Dodger Stadium since 1962. It is currently the third oldest stadium in the MLB.
